## 🌟 About Tinyville

Tinyville is a relaxing multiplayer world where every player contributes to a living economy.

Start with a small plot, gather resources from the surrounding lands, develop your skills, build your wealth, and become a respected member of the community.

Whether you're a fisherman, farmer, miner, crafter, merchant, or explorer, there's always something to do in Tinyville.

## 🌍 Shared World

Tinyville is designed around community interaction.

Every action contributes to the world:

```text
Fish → Trade
Farm → Craft
Mine → Build
Build → Sell
Sell → Grow
Grow → Expand
```

Players work together to create a vibrant and ever-evolving town.
